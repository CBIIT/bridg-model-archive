<p>View Description:<br/></p><p>This view shows the core classes associated with Documents in BRIDG.  Documents are critical to any protocol-driven research effort.  As a consequence, the THC elected to introduce into the BRIDG Model the core concepts around a 'document representation model' with the recognition that these concepts will be elaborated (and possibly extended) as additional content is brought to the THC for harmonization.  It is critical to recognize that the 'document framework' (shown in PURPLE in this view to indicate its "placeholder" status) represents a structural rather than semantic framework.  The structural model is conceptually drawn from the HL7 Clinical Document Architecture (CDA) specification and can be viewed as consisting of three 'levels' of structure, each with increasing levels of computationally interoperable semantics.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>As is the case with all classes, the THC introduces the concepts into the BRIDG Model in the hope of stimulating BRIDG community dialogue on the subject.<br/></p>
