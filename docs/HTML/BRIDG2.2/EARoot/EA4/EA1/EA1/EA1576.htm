<p>This is a relationship between an entity in a role and a participation in an act. This relationship asserts that an assessor may be represented by a single associated biologic entity. This relationship also asserts that an associated biologic entity may function as an assessor for zero to many observations. Note: an Assessor can be represented by one and only one of the following: a ClinicalResearchStaff or a Subject or an AssociatedBiologicEntity or a HealthcareProvider or a ClinicalResearchStaff or an OversightCommittee or a Laboratory or a Device.<br/></p>
