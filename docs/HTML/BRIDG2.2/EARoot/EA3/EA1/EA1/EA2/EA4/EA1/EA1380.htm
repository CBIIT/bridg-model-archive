Appears In: caBIG - AE-Triggered Schedule Change
<p>STORYBOARD:  Patient has a lab based AE which has been entered into caAERS.  This event would trigger caAERS to send a notification to study calendar that there maybe a change in patient schedule due to an AE-caused treatment delay.   A CRA who uses the PSC application may notice an alert when she tries to schedule this patient for the next treatment.  She will then have to take an action to ensure that the AE has been resolved before continuing treatment.  Then, if required, the patient’s schedule will be modified.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For Future:  Ideally SMEs would like to see [either caAERS pull lab values from caXchange (CTOM) or caXchange to push lab value to caAERS] to automatically determine which out of range values should result in an AE.  This step would overlap a planned feature for caAERS and would overlap the Load Lab Data scenario and the Identify Potential Dose Changes scenario which might lead to these scenarios being combined in a future phase.<br/></p>
