<p>An organization that devotes some or all of its resources (people, places, things) to the delivery of healthcare services (including the financial and administrative management of those resources).  A healthcare facility may be manifest as a single physical location (e.g. building), or, alternatively, as a distributed collection of physical spaces.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, Northwestern Memorial Hospital.<br/></p>
