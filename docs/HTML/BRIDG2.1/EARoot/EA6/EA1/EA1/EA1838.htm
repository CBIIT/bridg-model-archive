<p>An activity that is anticipated to occur at some time in the future. <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, pregnancy tests are planned for study subjects who are females of childbearing potential.  The pregnancy tests are in the future, and the particular women who will be tested have not been identified.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>NOTE: A planned activity could also be thought of as an activity at a particular stage in the business process in which the activities occur, i.e., in the "planned" stage rather than the "scheduled" stage or the "performed" stage. An instance of a planned activity is not assigned to a particular study subject, but to a "kind of" study subject. <br/></p>
