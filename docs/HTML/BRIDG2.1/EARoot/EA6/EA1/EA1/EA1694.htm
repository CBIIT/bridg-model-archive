<p>A path through the study which describes what activities the subject will be involved in as they pass through the study.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, a study could have 2 arms named IV-Oral and Oral-IV.  The name IV-Oral reflects a path that passes through IV treatment, then Oral treatment.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>NOTE: The group of subjects assigned to an Arm is often called a treatment group. <br/></p>
