Appears In: caBIG - Reconsent Patients
<p>STORYBOARD:  CRA would need to "reconsent" or notify all patients receiving trastuzamab (Drug x) containing regimens.  They need to identify all the studies that have trastuzamab as a study agent (from C3D/CDMS) than identify all patients for that study (C3PR).  The CRA then needs to verify that these patients are alive.  For patients who are alive, the system would add the "reconsent" activity in PSC for the next scheduled visit for all those patients.  <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Note:  this is a real scenario at most cancer centers; most of these processes are manual at cancer centers and the CRAs typically have to pull this together by querying various different applications.<br/></p>
