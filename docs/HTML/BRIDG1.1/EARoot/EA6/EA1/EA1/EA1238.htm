<p>An individual who participates in a clinical trial, either as recipient of the investigational product(s) or as a control.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, a participant can include a patient in an interventional CT, however in QOL trials the people in the trial are not receiving health care, but are participating in the observational study.  <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>NOTE: Participant is a role not a participation, and is similar to the notion of a patient. This includes potential patients to a clinical trial.<br/></p>
