<p>GFORGE BUG:  Remove overloaded attributes from Person class<br/></p><p>This class has many attributes that do not belong in a generalized Person class, but rather some specialization of Person, e.g., birthCountry, householdIncome, etc.  This may be an artifact of the harmonization with the HL7 RIM, where the philosophy was to define attributes as the highest level and exclude them from various specializations. Whereas, the philosophy of UML is to include only attributes at the highest level of generalization that apply to all specializations.<br/></p>
