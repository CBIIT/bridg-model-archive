<p>A set of information that describes what activities will be performed, when, and for which subjects.  Much of this information can be visualized as a table whose rows are PlannedActivities, and whose columns are PlannedSubjectStudyEncounters.  The cells in such a table are instances of PlannedSoACells.  <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Tabular representations of all or part of a Planned Schedule of Activities (SOA) are often included in protocol documents, and are sometimes called Schedules of Activities, Schedules of Events, or  Time and Events Tables.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, a Planned SOA might include information about tests planned to take place at particular clinic visits, the order of tests within visits, study drug the subject is to take and diary data they are to record while in a treatment StudySegment, and conditions under which study drug should be stopped.<br/></p>
