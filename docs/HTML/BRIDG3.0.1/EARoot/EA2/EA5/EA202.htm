public CD
  targetAnatomicSiteCode		 Map:CTOM=LesionDescription.anatomicSiteCode
Map:CTOM=LesionDescription.anatomicSiteCodeSystem
Notes: A coded value specifying the anatomic location that is the focus of an observation result.<br /><br />For example, arm for skin rash.<br /><br />NOTE:  The target site of the result may be different than the target site of the activity (PerformedObservation) that generated it.  For example, a chest x-ray (observation) has the target site of chest while the result might show an infiltration in the left lower lobe of the lung (target site of result), or a dermatological exam may check the skin across the whole body (target site of observation) while the result might identify a rash on the right leg (target site of result).
