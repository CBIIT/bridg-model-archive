<p>The completed action of observing, monitoring, measuring or otherwise qualitatively or quantitatively gathering data or information about one or more aspects of a subject's, study subject's or experimental unit's physiologic or psychologic state.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, lab test, taking vital signs, physical exam, etc.<br/></p>
