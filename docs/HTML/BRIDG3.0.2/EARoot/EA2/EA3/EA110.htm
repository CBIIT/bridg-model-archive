<p>The Protocol Representation sub-domain is intended for those involved in the planning and design of a research protocol. The majority of business requirements have come from those involved in clinical trial protocols.  It focuses on the characteristics of a study and the definition and association of activities within the protocols, including "arms" and "epochs".  It also includes the definitions of the roles that participate in those activities.<br/></p>
