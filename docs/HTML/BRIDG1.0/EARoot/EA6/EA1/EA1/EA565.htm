<p>A planned subject path through the study, described by a sequence of Elements. The group of subjects assigned to an Arm is typically equivalent to a treatment group.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, a study could have 3 Arms named Placebo, Treatment A, and Treatment B. Each of those arms could include 3 Epochs -- screening, intervention, and follow-up.  The screening Epoch may be identical for all three arms, but the intervention Epoch would have different activities for placebo, treatment A, and treatment B.<br/></p>
