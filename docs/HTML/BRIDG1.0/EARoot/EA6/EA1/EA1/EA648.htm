<p>A business process state of a study (represented here as a subtype) that is a collector of planned activities in a study including a description of the planned number of study subjects and the duration of their participation in the study.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>A planned study theoretically occurs before a study starts.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>A planned study is usually submitted to an IRB or sponsoring organization for review and approval before the study is executed.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>NOTE: For the purpose of Release 1, this is a collector of planned activities. Study and StudyProtocol include additional concepts including ( design, methodology, statistical considerations and organization).<br/></p>
