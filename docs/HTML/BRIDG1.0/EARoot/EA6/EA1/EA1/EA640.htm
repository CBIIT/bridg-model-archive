<p>An individual who participates in a clinical trial, either as recipient of the investigational product(s) or as a control.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>A human being who may be assigned to a study. The study specifies how the subject’s illness will be treated and/or monitored overtime.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, a participant can include a patient in an interventional CT, however in QOL trials the people in the trial are not receiving health care, but are participating in the observational study.  Participant is a role not participation, and is similar to the notion of a patient. Potential patients to a clinical trial.<br/></p>
